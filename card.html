Thank You Letter

Dear [classmate girl's name],

Firstly, I want to say that I deeply regret not having talked to you or considered you as a friend throughout the year. It was never your fault, and I realize now that I missed out on getting to know a kind and friendly person.

If there's one thing I've learned from this experience, it's that we should always be kind and considerate towards others, even if we don't know them well.

Even though I know that I cannot give this letter to you in person, I want you to know that writing this has been a cathartic experience for me. I will keep this letter as a reminder of the lessons I have learned, and as a memory of you as a kind and friendly person.

I regret not having the courage to speak to you and get to know you better, but I hope that someday I will be able to overcome my shyness and become a better person. For now, I want to thank you for being a positive influence in my life, even if it was in a small way.

I wish you all the best in your future, and I hope that our paths will cross again someday.

With gratitude, [Your name]
